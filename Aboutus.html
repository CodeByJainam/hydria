About Us


With a heritage that extends beyond 37 years, RUBYRUCY Group represents a narrative of improving lives, driving innovation, and making a lasting impact worldwide. RUBYRUCY Group is a leading conglomerate specializing in a broad spectrum of industrial and consumer products underpinned by a commitment to quality, innovation, and operational excellence.


RUBYRUCY is a leading manufacturer of Textile processing stenter machine spare parts such as Rail, Main drive gear box, Outlet desk, Radiator (Heat-Exchangers) in India. With a presence in 3 countries, we are a strong force in innovation and focus on our customers. Our companies have grown into world leaders in essential industries.

Hydria Premier Water is one of the distinguished products offered by RUBYRUCY Group. Hydria has always been dedicated to giving back to both the community and the environment. The concept of providing essential products like bottled water stemmed from the realization that millions of people still lack access to safe and pure drinking water.

Hydria is a naturally alkaline mineral water enriched completely by nature. We don’t add any minerals, Hydria is naturally filtered through layers of silt, clay, and rock, absorbing essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, sodium, silica, bicarbonates, and electrolytes from the earth’s crust. This natural process gives Hydria its alkaline properties. We've also included video clips of our farm (the source of Hydria) on our website, so you can enjoy a glimpse of the beautiful natural surroundings.

We have tested the water quality of over 500 brands and documented the results. Based on this analysis, we can confidently say that Hydria stands out as the highest quality water among all leading brands. With a strong presence across various sectors, including hospitality, aviation, catering, railways and modern trade, Hydria Water has become the trusted choice for discerning clients who prioritize purity, reliability and exceptional quality. Experience the superior quality and purity that defines Hydria Water the ultimate standard in packaged drinking natural mineral water.
