About Us

Our Legacy & Commitment to Purity

The RUBYRUCY Group Legacy

With a heritage that extends beyond 37 years, RUBYRUCY Group represents a narrative of improving lives, driving innovation, and making a lasting impact worldwide. We are a leading conglomerate specializing in industrial and consumer products, underpinned by a commitment to quality and operational excellence.


From manufacturing textile processing machinery parts to growing into world leaders in essential industries, our presence spans 3 countries. Hydria Premier Water is our distinguished offering, stemming from the realization that millions still lack access to safe, pure drinking water.

Why Hydria?

Hydria is a naturally alkaline mineral water enriched completely by nature. We don’t add minerals artificially. Hydria is naturally filtered through layers of silt, clay, and rock in the earth's crust, absorbing essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, sodium, silica, and electrolytes.


We have tested the water quality of over 500 brands. Based on this analysis, Hydria stands out as the highest quality water among leading brands. Whether for hospitality, aviation, or modern trade, Hydria is the trusted choice for those who prioritize purity.
