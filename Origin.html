Origin


Hydria’s journey began 670 million years ago long before the Himalayas or the Alps existed in the heart of the ancient Aravalli range, one of the oldest fold mountain systems on Earth. This is the story of the majestic Aravallis, a legend shaped by time, and the origin of Hydria.

For centuries, nature has been quietly crafting mineral-rich, alkaline water deep within this timeless landscape. Drop by drop, over a span of 20 years, water from the catchment area seeps through layers of alluvium and clay—natural filters that purify and enrich it before settling in our deep underground reservoirs.

It took time, research, and careful exploration to uncover this rare source. After mapping its origins in the Aravallis, we began bottling this natural treasure to bring you wellness in its purest form.

Launched in 2023, Hydria is officially certified under IS 13428 and presented with a distinct identity and thoughtfully designed packaging. Our bottling process meets the highest international standards, preserving Hydria’s natural mineral composition and ensuring purity in every sip. Each bottle of Hydria helps conserve 3 litres of bottled water. Our water aquifer is naturally recharged every 90 days during the rainy season each year.

In a person’s life, three things are essential for long-term health: pure air, pure water, and organic food. When we regularly consume these three, our overall fitness improves, allowing us to stay active and productive for longer. However, in today’s world, organic food is difficult to find, clean air is becoming rare, and pure water is not easily accessible. As a result, medicines have become a regular part of our lives and even our expenses.

If we want to reduce our dependence on medicine, we must prioritize and focus on these three pillars of health. By doing so, we can maintain lasting fitness, work more efficiently, and support our personal growth.

At Rubyrucy Beverages (Hydria), we understand the importance of these fundamentals. That’s why we’ve discovered and tapped into a natural source of mineral-rich water and started bottling it with care. You’re welcome to check the quality of our water yourself or have it tested by any certified laboratory. Nature has already given us everything we need — it's up to us to recognize its value and choose purity.
