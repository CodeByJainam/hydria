The Origin Story

670 Million Years in the Making

Hydria’s journey began 670 million years ago long before the Himalayas existed, in the heart of the ancient Aravalli range. For centuries, nature has been quietly crafting mineral-rich, alkaline water deep within this timeless landscape.


Drop by drop, over a span of 20 years, water from the catchment area seeps through layers of alluvium and clay—natural filters that purify and enrich it before settling in our deep underground reservoirs.

The Three Pillars of Health: Pure Air, Pure Water, and Organic Food. At Rubyrucy Beverages (Hydria), we focus on the second pillar. We have tapped into a natural source of mineral-rich water and bottle it with care. Nature has given us everything; we just deliver it to you.
